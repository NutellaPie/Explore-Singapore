Victoria Theatre & Concert Hall

About

The Victoria Theatre & Victoria Concert Hall was established in 1862. It now remains as a national monument of Singapore and is one of Singapore's oldest performing arts venue. The building, containing a 614-seat Theatre and a 673-seat Concert Hall, is located in the heart of Singapore's city centre.

The Victoria Theatre & Concert Hall has played a role in Singapore's history for over 150 years. It continues to be an exciting platform, supporting the growth of Singapore's arts industry.

Restored to its original neo-classical 1905 structure

Having undergone a 4-year refurbishment in 2010 which totaled to an amount of $158 million, the Victoria Theatre & Victoria Concert Hall saw new state-of-the-art facilities and amenities. Additionally, two smaller rooms for music, dance and theatre rehearsals was added.
