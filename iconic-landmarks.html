As Singapore continues to grow economically, the government has more captial on hand to build more iconic landmarks in our island city. One example would be The Jewel at Changi Airport, a retail destination which features state-of-the-art facilities that makes it eco-friendly. The said architecture is schedule for opening in 2019.

Without further ado, allow me to introuce some must-visit landmarks in Singapore!

Iconic Landmarks

Marina Bay

Explore Singapore's beautiful archetectiture all in one area. Ranging from a lotus shaped museum to a plateau resting above 3 buildings that resembles a boat, the Central Business District in Singapore is sure to put you in awe!

Singapore Flyer

Standing just 2.6 metres behind the tallest ferris wheel in the world, get a bird's eye view of Singapore right in the heart of our tiny island.

Henderson Waves Bridge

Take a walk on the tallest pedestrain bridge in Singapore at the Henserson Waves Bridge. A must-visit attraction if you are interested in nature as you'll be surround by lush greenery!
