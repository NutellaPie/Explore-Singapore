Spend time connecting with nature

Nature lovers fret not! With Singapore being a garden city, you will be able to explore
Singapore nature by visiting the places below! There are also places for animal lovers too!

Gardens by the Bay
Supertrees
Cloud Forest
Flower Dome

Discover a whole new world of plants at Gardens by the Bay which offers a large variety of flora. It also features the world's largest glass greenhouse, the flower dome spanning over 1.28 hectares of flowers and plants from the Mediterranean and semi-arid regions under glass. Enter into a mysterius world enveloped by mist at the cloud forest where you can take in a breath-taking mountain view surrounded by hidden floral gems. Not to forget, the manificent solar-powered supertrees that converts sunlight into energy with photovoltaic systems to provide lighting and to aid in water technology for the conservatories below.
Location: 18 Marina Gardens Drive, Singapore 018953

Botanic Gardens

Singapore Botanic Gardens, being more than 150 years old, it is the first and only UNESCO site and the oldest garden in the city. It posses a wide array of botanical and horticultural attractions with a rich history and a wonderful plant collection of worldwide significance. It is advisable for you to check out the Singapore Botanic Gardens Heritage Museum, Orchid Garden and if you are lucky you may also be able to enjoy free concerts at the Shaw foundation symphony stage. Not to worry about being hungry or thristy, there are cafes and restaurants there to satisfy your hunger and thirst!
Location: 1 Cluny Rd, Singapore 259569

Singapore Zoo

Animal lovers would enjoy a day at the Singapore Zoo that hosts 2800 animals of 300 species of which 26% are endangered. Visitors will be able to learn more about the animals and thier habitats and participate in activities such as feeding the giraffes, going on wildlife tours and of course sit back and relax to watch animal shows when you need a break from walking! Be sure to check out the different show timings!
Location: 80 Mandai Lake Rd, 729826
