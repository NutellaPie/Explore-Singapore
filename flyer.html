About

Standing at 165 metres, this giant observation wheel offers 360-degree city views of Singapore and parts of Malaysia and Indonesia on a clear day. As Asia’s largest observation wheel, the Singapore Flyer offers a different visitor experience in the day and night.

Marvel at spectacular panoramas of the city skyline from the Singapore Flyer.

Located at the heart of Marina Bay, prepared to be awed by the magnificent view of the city skyline as you stand 42 stories high. Being Asia's largest ferris wheel, the iconic wheel features 28 fully air-conditioned glass capsules, each able to transport you on a 30-minute journey of stunning day and night scenes.

From your capsule on top of the world, feast your eyes on iconic and historical landmarks and views from Marina Bay to the Singapore River, such as Raffles Place, Merlion Park, Empress Place and the Padang. With any luck, you’ll be able to spot landmarks up to 45 kilometres away, like Changi Airport, Sentosa Island and parts of Malaysia and Indonesia.

The best timem to go for a ride on the Singapore Flyer is when dusk falls. The skyscrapers are blanketed by the golden sun and when night falls, the darkness is illuminated by the buildings’ twinkling lights.

Wine and dine at the world’s first full butler sky dining experience.

Be it Valetine's day or any other event, bring your loved ones to the Singapore Flyer for a sumptuous meal which includes a four-course menu, personalised butler service, wine pairing options and of course, amazing skyline views. Details of the prices can be seen in the table below.

How to get there

Map of the general area

By Bus:

Take bus 36, 70M, 97, 97e, 106, 111, 133, 162M, 502, 502A, 518, 518A, 700A, 857 and alight at "Opp The Ritz-Carlton" (Bus Stop ID: 02171)

By Train:

Board the Circle Line and alight at Promenade Station (CC4/DT15). Take Exit A and walk 464 metres towards the Singapore Flyer.

By Cab:

Address: 30 Raffles Ave, Singapore 039803. Use code "tayr644ue" to enjoy SGD$3 off all Uber rides.
